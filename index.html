How to Use This App

1. Connect your Muse 2 — Tap the Connect button on the Home tab. Make sure your headband is on and Bluetooth is enabled.

2. Your brain is read — EEG data streams across 5 bands (Delta, Theta, Alpha, Beta, Gamma). The app diagnoses your current neurological state and prescribes a frequency.

3. Listen — The oscillator plays your prescribed frequency as a sine tone. Use the toggle to turn it on/off and the volume slider to adjust. It won't restart by itself once you turn it off.

4. Explore the tabs — Home: live prescription and EEG oscilloscope. Cymatics: 3D sacred geometry. Biofield: toroidal energy visualization. Flower: 27-breath meditation. History: session logs.

No Muse? You can explore without one. Go to Cymatics, tap any frequency, and toggle the oscillator to hear it.
